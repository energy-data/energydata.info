Featured Datasets

  1. East Africa - Renewable Energy Zones Solar PV (2015)

    Published by Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory

    This spatial vector dataset shows areas of identified high quality potential for Solar Photovoltaic (PV) development divided into large contiguous areas called "zones." This dataset contains zones for the Eastern Africa Power Pool (EAPP) region.

    View dataset

  2. Nigeria - Kano Electricity Distribution Company - Grid Map (2016)

    Published by Columbia University Earth Institute

    Data collected as input to the geospatial least-cost planning for universal electricity access by 2030 developed as part of the ESMAP funded World Bank Nigeria Electricity Access Project (NEAP).
